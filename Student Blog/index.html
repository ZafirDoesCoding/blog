Places Must Visit

Sentosa

Visited May 2023

Sentosa is a wonderland full of many attractions and hotels. The many attractions at the island include Universal Studios Singapore, Wings of Time and the Siloso Point. Take the cable car to and from the different landmarks around and to the mainland, where you can take the subway to the city. The Sentosa Express monorail provides access to VivoCity, a large shopping mall. So try not to miss this heck of a place during your Singapore trip

Gardens by the Bay

Visited May 2023

You've left Sentosa to visit somewhere near the city. The place your looking for is the Gardens by the Bay, a colourful garden with enormous "supertrees" connected by a walkway. Do not miss the sunset here, the supertrees will burst into bloom and colour, and at 2030 hours every night is the Garden Rhapsody Show, where opera music is played as the supertrees change colour. Make sure to shortlist this on your wishlist.

My Hobbies

Cricket
Bat and ball sport

Cricket is a challenging but enjoyable game that can be played almost anywhere. All you need is a bat and a ball. The aim is for the batting team is to get as many runs as possible and avoid the other team from passing the score. The game is challenging, with cries for catching and run outs. If you catch the ball, the batter is out, or if the bowler hits the stumps with the ball. It is especially fun at night, when the ball is hit, you do not know where it has gone!
